History of Matcha

Matcha originated in China during the Tang Dynasty but was perfected in Japan. Zen Buddhist monks used matcha to stay alert during long meditation sessions. Over centuries, it became an essential part of Japanese tea ceremonies, symbolizing purity, harmony, and tranquility.
